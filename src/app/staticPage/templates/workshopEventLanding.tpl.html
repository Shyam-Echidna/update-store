April 21 'Art in Bloom' Floral Design Workshop

Art in Bloom is a four-day festival of fresh floral arrangements inspired by fine art, presented by the Friends of the Institute at the Minneapolis Institute of Arts. Attend this special evening for a closer look at one of this year’s signature pieces, “Laurel Tree of Carolina” by Mark Catesby, and then learn how to create an arrangement inspired by that very artwork. Ticket expense covers all of the evening’s activities and instruction, lights bites, and the materials used for your take-home floral creation. Class time is Thursday, April 21, 6-8 p.m. One person may attend per ticket. No tickets will be mailed. Please check in at the gift counter at Bachman's on Lyndale on the day of the event. Note: Lyndale events are handicap accessible. Please notify ekjar@bachmans.com in advance, so that we can advise and assist. POLICIES: Please see our Returns & Exchanges page for policies on ticketed events. Workshops are designed for adults and children aged 13 or older. A ticket is required for each participant. April 7 Ideas House Ticket

EEnjoy a self-guided visit to Bachman's Spring Ideas House located in our historic family home at Bachman's on Lyndale Ave. Each season, our designers transform the house into an inspiring destination for seasonal trends and repeatable ideas. They completely repaint, refurnish, and redecorate the house with the latest product and trend ideas in home décor and entertaining, using their signature mix of vintage and new pieces. PLAN YOUR VISIT: You will NOT receive a ticket in the mail. On the day of your visit, pick up your ticket(s) at the gift counter in our Lyndale store (6010 Lyndale Ave. S., Minneapolis). Tickets are good for a 30-minute self-guided tour starting at the time of the ticket purchased. PRICING NOTES: With your ticket, you will receive a coupon good for $5 off a $25 purchase at any of our six Bachman’s Floral, Gift and Garden Centers. 20% of Ideas House admissions will be donated to Friends of the Institute, the MIA's volunteer organization. Free admission for children 12 and under. Tickets are not refundable; see our Returns and Exchanges page for details. ACCESSIBILITY: Because the Ideas House is situated on an historic property, it does not include full handicap accessibility. The main floor of the house is accessible via an outdoor wheelchair ramp. However, the second floor is only accessible by the home's original indoor staircase. No strollers please. We apologize for any inconvenience.
