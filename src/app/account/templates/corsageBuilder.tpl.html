Design Recommendations

When ordering formal flowers, consider the following

  1. Dress Color: Pick flower colors that complement the color of your dress.
  2. Flower Type: Look for the types of flowers that most reflect the theme of the occasion, your personality or dress style.
  3. Wristlet Style: Choose a wristlet that best matches your stile of dress and coordinate with your other accessories.
  4. Personality: Consider adding extra "bling" to your corsage such as gems, a wristlet or ribbon.
  5. Matching Boutonnière: It's easiest to match his Boutonnière to your chosen corsage, rather than the other way around.
