Terrariums are unique ecosystems, constructed in a clear glass container. Natural elements meet creative aesthetics, to bring an alluring environment to life. Put together your own craetive visions with a little guidance from a Bachman's expert and learn how to design a terrarium with miniature plants and a mix of natural materials evocative of the sea.

Practical considerations and design elements will be discussed. Ticket cost covers all materials needed, participants may bring small objects of interest to personalize their botanical creation. One person may attend class workshop per ticket.
